Competitive Challenge · Objection Handling
Handle Objections With Confidence
Customer Says
"The CR-V is a lot like RAV4 but with a lower price."
Your Response
  • RAV4 has more horsepower in every configuration
  • RAV4's touchscreen is larger — standard
  • More electrified powertrain flexibility (HEV + PHEV across grades)
  • Expanded grade choices and greater performance makes the difference
Customer Says
"The Rogue's interior seems nicer."
Your Response
  • Nissan has done well with their interiors — acknowledge it
  • RAV4 balances interior comfort with more horsepower, advanced tech, and electrified options
  • RAV4's TAMM system is more capable and future-ready
Customer Says
"Kia Sportage has a longer warranty, more tech, and lower starting price."
Your Response
  • Lower entry price = stripped standard features (BSM/RCTA not standard on Sportage)
  • RAV4 is value-packed from the entry grade
  • Toyota's history of retaining value over time — KBB Best Resale
  • 1,200+ dealerships vs Kia's service network
