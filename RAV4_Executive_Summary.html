The Opportunity
Training reaches dealers. Measurement doesn't.
Toyota invests significantly in developing world-class product and competitive training content. The Meeting-in-a-Box program delivers that content effectively — but once a PDF leaves corporate, there is no visibility into whether it was used, retained, or had any impact on the sales floor. There is no system today that tells us who was trained, what they learned, or where knowledge gaps exist by region or dealer. The Sales Coach Engine closes that loop.
The Solution
Three components. One connected system.
01 · Presenter
Live Session Display
A cinematic, TV-ready presentation that guides managers through the 5-phase Sales Coach Engine. Timer-controlled, keyboard-navigable, with built-in speaker notes.
02 · Quiz
Mobile Knowledge Check
Salespeople scan a QR code on their phone during the session and take a 15-question timed quiz. Covers both product knowledge and competitive objection handling.
03 · Dashboard
Analytics & Reporting
Real-time leaderboards, regional breakdowns, dealer rankings, certification tracking, and CSV export — accessible at local, regional, and national levels.

The Ask
What We're Requesting
Approval to run a 90-day pilot in one region using the Sales Coach Engine alongside the existing RAV4 Meeting-in-a-Box program. No budget for software. No vendor contract. No infrastructure change. We are asking for time, access to one regional training coordinator, and permission to measure.
